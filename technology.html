The Technology

Together with NASA, a novel radiation shielding has been developed, to overcome radiation challenges for spacecraft equipment and other electronic components affected by ionising radiation. Most radiation shielding we have today are developed based on Aluminium based composites. However, these novel state-of-the art shielding advanced materials are based on the atomic number Z.
